# Design System Documentation

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system.

## Theme Overview

The current theme is characterized by a **dark color mode**, emphasizing depth and contrast.

## Color Palette

Our brand utilizes a distinct color palette to evoke a modern and dynamic feel.

*   **Primary Color:** `#A29BFE` - A vibrant purple, serving as the main brand color for calls to action and key interactive elements.
*   **Secondary Color:** `#2D3436` - A deep, almost black tone, used for supporting UI elements and secondary actions.
*   **Tertiary Color:** `#00CEC9` - A bright turquoise, providing an accent for highlights and decorative elements.
*   **Neutral Color:** `#0F0F1B` - A very dark grey, forming the base for backgrounds and surfaces, ensuring legibility in dark mode.

## Typography

We maintain a consistent typographic hierarchy across all components.

*   **Headlines:** `Manrope` - Chosen for its modern and geometric feel, providing strong visual impact.
*   **Body Text:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` - Selected for its excellent readability and contemporary appeal, suitable for longer content blocks.
*   **Labels:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` - Ensures consistency with body text for smaller, functional text elements.

## Shape and Form

The visual language incorporates a moderate level of roundedness for UI elements, striking a balance between softness and structure.

*   **Roundedness:** `2` (Moderate roundedness) - Providing a friendly yet professional aesthetic without being overly soft or sharp.

## Spacing

Our design system utilizes a normal level of spacing to ensure clarity and visual comfort across all interfaces.

*   **Spacing:** `2` (Normal) - This ensures elements are well-separated without feeling sparse or cramped, contributing to a clean layout.
